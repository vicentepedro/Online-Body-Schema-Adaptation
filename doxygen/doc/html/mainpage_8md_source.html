1 # Online Body Schema Adaptation & Markerless eye-hand kinematic calibration
2 
3 We propose a markerless hand pose estimation software for the iCub humanoid robot.
4 The agent can calibrate its eye-hand kinematic chain without any markers which, from a developmental psychology perspective, can be seen as a body schema adaptation.
